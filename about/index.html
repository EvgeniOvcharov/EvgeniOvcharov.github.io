My name is Evgeni Ovcharov. I have a Ph.D. in mathematics from the University of Edinburgh in 2009. I have worked as a researcher in Heidelberg University, Germany, 2010 - 2014, the Heidelberg Institute for Theoretical Studies, 2014 - 2015, and the Institute of Mathematics and Informatics at the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, 2016 - present. I am interested in mathematical statistics, machine learning, programming, mathematical modelling in sports, statistical forecasting, financial time series and more...
